VALORANT

In February 2021, Paper Rex entered the VALORANT competitive scene, signing the original CS:GO team as the first iteration of the roster. Although the roster had a rough season, they learned a lot as a team. During the 2022 season, our roster was top-notch, securing 1st place in both stages of the APAC(Asia-Pacific) Challengers. Furthermore, our roster had a amazing performance at international tournaments, placing 4th and 2nd at both Masters tournaments. Our roster had a rough start coming into the 2023 season, placing 17th-32th in the LOCK//IN international tournament but we quickly picked things back up, securing 1st place in the Pacific League, 3rd at Masters Tokyo, and 2nd at Champions 2023.
