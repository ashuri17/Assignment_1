Our Partners

YOUC1000
Paper Rex has partnered with Indonesia's well-known beverage company, YOUC1000! With this partnership, it will be able to rehydrate our players with Vitamin C and restore electrolytes. This ensures our players are energized and healthy while practising for tournaments.

HyperX
Paper Rex has joined up with HyperX, a popular American company due to their high quality peripherals. This partnership ensures our players get their hands on top-notch peripherals, upgrading their gaming experience and ensuring that the players showcase their best performance in future tournaments!

Dreamcore
Paper Rex links up with Dreamcore,a fellow Singaporean company who are popular for their computer systems. With this, our players will be equipped with systems that are customized to their needs, and without any worry of their systems crashing.

SecretLab
Founded 9 years ago, SecretLab is very popular for their design in gaming chairs. Paper Rex partners up with SecretLab in order to ensure utmost comfortability for our players during practice sessions. With SecretLab'saward-winning chairs, the chair's features like Multi-Tilt Mechanism and Adjustable Arm Rests ensures that our players' needs are met.
